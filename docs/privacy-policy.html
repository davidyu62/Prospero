Privacy Policy

Prospero · Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prospero ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the App. Prospero is an information and analytics tool that displays cryptocurrency and macroeconomic market data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Prospero does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information. We do not collect your financial account details, and the App does not execute trades or hold any funds.

2. Information Collected Automatically

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve and measure ads, this third-party service may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers, including the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), where permitted;
  • Advertising and usage data, such as ad interactions and general app-usage signals;
  • Coarse device and technical information (for example, device type and operating system version).

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's own privacy practices. We do not control how Google uses this information. You can review Google's policies here: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, if the App or its advertising partners request to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies, you will be shown Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. You may grant or deny this permission at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. Market Data

Cryptocurrency and macroeconomic figures shown in the App are retrieved from our backend service and third-party data providers. This data is public market information and does not include any personal information about you.

5. How Information Is Used

The limited data collected automatically is used only to display and measure advertising and to keep the App functioning correctly. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

Prospero is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain personal user profiles. Any advertising-related data is retained by Google according to Google's retention policies, not by us.

8. Your Choices

  • You can limit ad tracking by denying the App Tracking Transparency prompt or by adjusting your device's privacy settings.
  • You can reset your advertising identifier from your device settings at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
